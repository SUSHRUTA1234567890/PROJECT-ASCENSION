# PROJECT ASCENSION

A cosmic horror game built with Three.js and HTML5. Experience the terror of consciousness integration with Project Ascension.

## Game Overview

You wake up in a facility with no memory. Your objective is simple: reach the reactor core. But nothing is as it seems. The logs tell a different story—one of consciousness being absorbed into a quantum network.

**Features:**
- First-person exploration through labyrinthine hallways
- Flickering atmospheric lighting
- Interactive computer terminals with horror logs
- Keycard puzzle system
- Multiple endings based on choices
- Cosmic horror narrative

## How to Play

1. **Start the game** by clicking "ENTER FACILITY"
2. **Move** using WASD keys
3. **Look around** by moving your mouse (click to lock mouse)
4. **Find the keycard** - explore and interact with objects
5. **Read terminals** - click on green computer screens for the story
6. **Reach the reactor core** - the blue glowing sphere at the end
7. **Discover your fate** - experience the ending sequence

## Controls

- **WASD** - Move forward/backward/left/right
- **Mouse** - Look around (click to lock/unlock)
- **E** - Close terminal window
- **Click** - Interact with terminals and objects
- **Shift** - Sprint (movement boost)
